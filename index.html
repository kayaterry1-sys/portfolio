About Me

Senior Copy and Brand Leader with over ten years' experience across communications, journalism, consumer media, beauty, lifestyle and purpose-led organisations. I specialise in shaping brand narratives, leading integrated campaigns and translating complex information into clear, culturally relevant storytelling that drives engagement, trust and measurable impact.

Currently leading copy and messaging across Marketing and Communications at Air Ambulance Charity Kent Surrey Sussex (KSS), delivering national campaigns, flagship publications and high-profile partnerships, including a Netflix documentary collaboration. Previously held a senior editorial role at Hearst, where I led teams, launched new formats and delivered content at scale across print, digital, social and live platforms.

Before Hearst, I worked at MailOnline, the UK's most-read English-language news website, where I launched the live blog format following Russia's invasion of Ukraine in February 2022. I led strategy across social media and digital platforms for exclusive news stories, and served as the royal correspondent for the website, with a proven ability to identify designers and trends worn by members of the royal family that would resonate with global audiences.

I bring together creative thinking, editorial rigour and strategic insight. My strengths include campaign development, brand storytelling, digital strategy, stakeholder management and earned media. I thrive in fast-paced environments where culture, audience insight, and purpose meet, and I'm driven to create communications that strengthen brands and deliver meaningful impact.
