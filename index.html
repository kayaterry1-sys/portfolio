About Me

I'm a strategic and creative communications professional with over a decade of experience leading brand storytelling across publishing, digital media and the charity sector. My background in journalism gives me a sharp editorial instinct and an understanding of what resonates with audiences at scale.

I currently lead copy and messaging across Marketing and Communications at Air Ambulance Charity Kent Surrey Sussex (KSS), shaping campaigns that reach two million supporters. My work includes flagship outputs such as the supporter magazine Above and Beyond, the charity's first Impact Report, strategic partnerships including a documentary series with Netflix, and messaging for major fundraising campaigns. I also support crisis and reactive communications, ensuring clarity and consistency when it matters most.

Earlier in my career at Hearst, I held senior editorial roles, developing content to grow audiences and monetise brands. I conceptualised and launched Suddenly Single for Best magazine, increasing organic engagement by 40% in its first ten months. I also led Cosmopolitan's first TikTok live shopping event, achieving a 21% click-through rate and delivering a significant impact on both audience and commercial growth.

I bring together creative thinking, editorial rigour and strategic insight. My strengths include campaign development, brand storytelling, digital strategy, stakeholder management and earned media. I thrive in fast-paced environments where culture, audience insight, and purpose meet, and I'm driven to create communications that strengthen brands and deliver meaningful impact.
